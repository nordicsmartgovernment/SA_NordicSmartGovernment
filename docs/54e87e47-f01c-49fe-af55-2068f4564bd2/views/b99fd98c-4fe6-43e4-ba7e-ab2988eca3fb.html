Insurance ()
An insurance company might get the inventory of assets to insure directly from the asset management application function at the client-SME. However, this does not cover all use-cases for insurance. A special case is credit insurance, if this is relevant it would be a part of the "credit lending" processes. Also, insurance covering people (health, pensions, life etc) is not covered as this opens up a completely different set of challenges regarding handling of personally identifiable information.
